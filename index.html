MS Dhoni

Mahendra Singh Dhoni is an Indian professional cricketer who was captain of the Indian national cricket team in limited-overs formats from 2007-2017.

Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian international cricketer and former captain of the India national cricket team. He plays for RCB in the Indian Premier League.

Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma is an Indian international cricketer who is the current captain of the Indian national team. A right-handed batsman.

Sachin Tendulkar

Sachin Ramesh Tendulkar is an Indian former international cricketer who captained the Indian national team. He is one of the greatest batsmen.

Ravindra Jadeja

Ravindrasinh Anirudhsinh Jadeja, commonly known as Ravindra Jadeja, is an Indian international cricketer. He is a left arm, off spin bowling all-rounder.

KL Rahul

Kananur Lokesh Rahul is an Indian international cricketer. He is the current vice-captain of the India national cricket team in all formats.
